The Postcard

The core artifact. A full-bleed horizontal photo in a white card frame. Tap ⤺ to turn it over — caption on the left, a divider, and a postmark on the right. Drag any photo onto a card to fill it.

Share-out · vertical · export only

A composed portrait image for sharing out — nothing like the in-feed card. Both faces of the postcard sit over the Kairo, set quietly in the background, with the wordmark signed in the corner. The only place the prompt and logo ever appear. Drop a photo on the front.
